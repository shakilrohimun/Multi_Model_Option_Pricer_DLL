\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Structure representing a point on the yield curve. 

A \doxylink{struct_rate_point}{Rate\+Point} encapsulates a specific maturity value and its associated interest rate. The maturity is typically represented as a fraction (e.\+g., between 0 and 1) to denote the time in years.
