\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Structure grouping the \doxylink{struct_greeks}{Greeks} of an option. 

The \doxylink{struct_greeks}{Greeks} quantify the sensitivity of the option\textquotesingle{}s price to changes in different market parameters\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Delta\+: Sensitivity to changes in the underlying asset\textquotesingle{}s price.
\item Gamma\+: Rate of change of Delta with respect to the underlying asset\textquotesingle{}s price.
\item Vega\+: Sensitivity to changes in the underlying asset\textquotesingle{}s volatility.
\item Theta\+: Sensitivity to the passage of time (time decay).
\item Rho\+: Sensitivity to changes in the risk-\/free interest rate. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
